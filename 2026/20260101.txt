Examples don’t have to be good to be useful.
Bad behavior teaches you !!limits!!: what not to do, who not to become, how not to live.
If you lack good models, study the bad ones carefully.
Intelligence is learning even from what repels you.
[Failure](post.html#2026/20260105.txt) is often just that lesson made unavoidable.
