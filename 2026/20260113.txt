Boredom can [trigger](https://ivana-rep.github.io/thoughtcapsules/post.html?p=2026/20260115.txt) the worst behaviors.
That’s why I’ve learned to fear it.
But boredom is also necessary.
Not as fuel for creativity or productivity,
but as space to rest and reset.
It sits close to [anxiety](post.html#2026/20260106.txt).
And it pushes the mind to chase [relief](post.html#2026/20260111.txt), no matter the cost.
