Most people can access extra resources when they’re under pressure.
Very few can do it in normal conditions.
Those who can don’t rely on chaos to function.
If you need a crisis to perform at your best, you’re not performing.
You’re reacting.
That’s the Red Queen Effect: running faster just to [stay in place](post.html?p=2026/20260127.txt).
The real goal is different:
!!to access your best self when everything is calm!!.

\ The ultimate measure of a man is not where he stands in moments of comfort and convenience, but where he stands at times of challenge and controversy.
