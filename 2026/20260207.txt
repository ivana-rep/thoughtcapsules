Sequences help. They become a healthy version of [autopilot](post.html?p=2026/20260116.txt) 
Routines too, but this is slightly different.
This applies to every [decision](post.html?p=2026/20260110.txt), not just repeated tasks.
Pair habits with cues, not just times.
Automate where possible, to reduce room for derailment.
Remove friction for the same reason.
Make the choice easy.
