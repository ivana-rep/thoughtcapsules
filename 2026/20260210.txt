Stoicism is widely misinterpreted today.
It’s often presented as a push toward indifference.
Emotional numbness as a virtue.
That’s not Stoicism.
Stoicism isn’t the erasure of emotions or attachments.
It’s their rational management.
!!Not feeling less.
Feeling without being ruled by it!!.
