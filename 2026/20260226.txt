I'm trying to convince myself of the importance of journaling.
For it to be useful, it must not become [rumination on paper](post.html?p=2026/20260213.txt).
The goal isn’t to correct the thoughts in your mind in order to improve.
Change comes when you detach from yourself, placing distance between the experience and the observer, watching it from that distance.
!!Processes!!, not identity.
The mind should learn to watch itself at work so that reactivity decreases, compulsions lose strength, and transformation becomes the result of sustained attention, not willpower.
