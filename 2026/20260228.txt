\ The history of every day.
\ What is the history of every day in your case?
\ Look at your habits that constitute it:
\ are they the product of innumerable little cowardices and lazinesses,
\ or of your courage and inventive reason?
\ Friedrich Nietzsche –  Die fröhliche Wissenschaft
