You photograph the place not to remember it.
You document that you were there.
This is what Moeller and D'Ambrosio call profilicity: the profile precedes and constitutes the experience.
[Performative travel](post.html?p=2026/20260224.txt) is one symptom. It goes much further.
!!The profile isn't the record. It's the thing!!.
Not a pathology to correct. A paradigm to understand.
