A folk tale: a merchant from Swaffham dreams of treasure in London, walks there, and meets a stranger who has dreamed of treasure in Swaffham — in the merchant's own garden.
He goes home and digs it up.
Book knowledge is the stranger in London.
It points you back to what was already there.
!!But you had to walk first!!.
[Wisdom comes from friction](post.html?p=2026/20260201.txt). Reading alone can't provide it.
