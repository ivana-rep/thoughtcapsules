At some point, Infinite Jest stopped being a novel.
It became an identity signal: carry it to look ambitious, mock it to look knowing.
Either way, not reading — using.
Thirty years later, readers are finding the actual book.
They're surprised. It's funny, it's warm, it's direct.
!!The distance between a work's reputation and its content is its own subject!!.
It tells you more about [identity objects](post.html?p=2026/20260222.txt) than about the work.
