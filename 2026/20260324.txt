The skeptic's move: things look different from different positions, so what you perceive must be a mental representation, not the thing itself.
But perspective isn't the same as interposition. A road looks different from each end. That doesn't mean you're not standing on the road.
!!The jump from "things appear" to "therefore we perceive representations" is the weak link!! — and it's [load-bearing](post.html?p=2026/20260320.txt).
