Consuming information isn't thinking. The difference between accumulating content and developing thought isn't the quantity of input — it's what happens after.
The step that matters: take an idea and ask what it means for you, not for whoever formulated it. Without it, understanding stays abstract. You can hold an idea while it changes nothing — using it as an [identity signal](post.html?p=2026/20260314.txt), not actually reading it.
!!The real test: what would you do differently now?!! If nothing, you haven't understood it. You've just held it.
