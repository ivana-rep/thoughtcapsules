Letting the wrong person become the face of a legitimate idea is a slow surrender. The ideas don't get discredited — they get contaminated. The content doesn't change; what changes is the company it keeps. That's [signal overriding content](post.html?p=2026/20260314.txt).
The instinct is to abandon the territory. But the idea preexists the spokesman.
!!The counter-move isn't retreat. It's reclaiming the same ground, to a higher standard.!!
