Nostalgia doesn't recall the past. It reconstructs it: selectively, charitably, with the friction edited out.
The danger isn't the looking back. It's using what you find there as evidence. !!What you miss isn't what was. It's [the version you built afterward](post.html?p=2026/20260127.txt).!!
