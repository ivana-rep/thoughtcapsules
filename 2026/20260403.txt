Fluency and understanding are different capacities. You can produce correct output without knowing what makes it correct. Language is the obvious case, but the split runs through every skill.
!!Fluency is [pattern recognition](post.html?p=2026/20260305.txt). Understanding is knowing why the pattern holds.!!
