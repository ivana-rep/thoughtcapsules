2026_04_07 | Permitted facts

↳ [back to index](index.html)

Some arguments don't evaluate evidence — they start from the conclusion and [work backward](post.html?p=2026/20260307.txt) to decide what can be considered. If a fact would be uncomfortable, it becomes suspect. If an inquiry might produce the wrong answer, the inquiry becomes impermissible. The standard has been replaced by the verdict. !!The risk isn't that wrong ideas spread. It's that the mechanism for evaluating any idea stops working.!!
