The unexpected detour is valuable because it wasn't planned. That's not incidental — that's the mechanism. Once you turn it into a system — track your side-quests, schedule your spontaneity — you replace the thing with its performance. Planning for unplannedness destroys what it tries to preserve. !!The [friction](post.html?p=2026/20260201.txt) was the point.!!
