You don't find yourself through enough introspection. You build yourself through enough choices. [The self](post.html?p=2026/20260404.txt) isn't a hidden thing waiting to be excavated — it's being assembled now, out of what you do and don't do. Envy is useful here: not as a wound to manage, but as a map. !!What you can't look at without that particular ache is telling you precisely what you want.!!
