Some forms of generosity are invisible because they consist entirely of restraint. Not sending the message. Arriving before you're needed. Keeping a [commitment](post.html?p=2026/20260221.txt) not because anyone is watching, but because someone is counting on it. Not making another person's difficulty about yourself. !!These leave no trace, which is part of what makes them generous.!!
