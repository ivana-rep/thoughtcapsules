Stability flattens behavior. In safe environments, organisms conserve energy — including the energy required to [deviate](post.html?p=2026/20260326.txt). The data follows: remakes dominate cinema, teenage drinking falls, the franchise beats the experiment. The creative and the criminal share the same substrate: risk tolerance, willingness to be wrong in public, defiance of expectation. !!Safety doesn't only protect you from danger. It also protects you from yourself.!!
