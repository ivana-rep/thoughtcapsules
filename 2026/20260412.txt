Certainty is efficient. It closes the question and moves you forward. [Curiosity](post.html?p=2026/20260125.txt) keeps the question open and tolerates not knowing. Feynman stopped trying to be productive and watched a plate spin. The insight came from that. You can't force the interesting thought. !!You can only stay available to it — which means staying uncertain long enough for something unexpected to happen.!!
