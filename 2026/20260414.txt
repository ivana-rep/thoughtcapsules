Even when you act against consensus, you still need someone to know. Not to agree. Just to witness. Conscience fails at this. "A coward," Goldsmith called it: too easily won over by whoever is arguing. !!The need for a [witness](post.html?p=2026/20260310.txt) who is neither yourself nor your allies is structural to moral life, not ornamental.!!
