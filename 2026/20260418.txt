The case for kindness doesn't require sentiment. It requires only accuracy: you don't know what the person in front of you is carrying. You never do. !!That gap between what's visible and what's real is large enough to justify a [default](post.html?p=2026/20260410.txt) of generosity.!! Unkindness assumes information it doesn't have.
