We are born isolated, aware of death, and constitutively at odds with the world. Staying coherent across a life is the remarkable achievement. What looked like stability in earlier eras was mostly scaffolding: fixed roles, religious certainty, family as fate. These structures [kept quiet](post.html?p=2026/20260310.txt) what is now diagnosed. !!The rise in diagnoses isn't more suffering. It's the same suffering, now legible.!!
