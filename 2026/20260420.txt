The quality of a choice depends less on the problem than on the state you bring to it. Exhaustion degrades judgment before the question arrives. This is a design constraint, not an excuse: build the substrate first. Then: failure plus reflection plus repetition. !!Each alone is inert. Together, they're how competence actually forms.!!
