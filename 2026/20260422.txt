There's a point where an environment stops fitting: a role, a friendship, a version of yourself. You feel it as discomfort before you can name it. The temptation is to read that discomfort as disloyalty. !!Outgrowing something isn't abandoning it. It's completing your relationship with it.!! Leave with gratitude. The next self forms in the space you clear.
