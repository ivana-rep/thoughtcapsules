↳ [back to index](index.html)

Not all decisions carry the same kind of risk. The useful question isn't "is this right?" but "can we correct it?" Reversible decisions deserve speed. Irreversible ones deserve weight. Most people apply the opposite: they overthink what can be undone and rush what cannot. !!The shape of the decision matters more than its content.!!
