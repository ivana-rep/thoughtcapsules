The race feels unwinnable because you're running on someone else's track. Switch the metric and the race changes. Most people compete vertically, trying to go higher in one defined hierarchy. The rarer move is horizontal: combine competencies no one else has combined. !!You become harder to replace not by being better at their game but by inventing your own.!! Persist long enough and the field thins by itself.
