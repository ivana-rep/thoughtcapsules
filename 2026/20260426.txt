Health, wealth, reputation: the Stoics called these indifferent. Not worthless, but not the point. The Greek word is khrēsis: use. !!Virtue is the use you make of indifferent things.!! The same wealth can be generous or hoarded. What you're given is the material. What you do with it is the work.
