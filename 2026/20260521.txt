Most people treat their thinking as a fixed asset. What's actually fixed is the baseline. What shifts with practice is how you use it: asking a better question, updating a stale assumption, choosing carefully what you let in. !!Thinking well is a [craft](post.html?p=2026/20260403.txt), not a talent.!!
