You don't know other people. You know the story you've built around them. Iris Murdoch called this fantasy: the ego casting others as characters in its own drama. The opposite is imagination. Unselfing: the effort to see someone as they actually are, not as your projection requires. You can [listen](post.html?p=2026/20260520.txt) to someone for an hour and come away with your original story intact. !!The story you write about someone is not them. It's the limit of your attention.!!
