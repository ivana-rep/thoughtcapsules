Pain is unavoidable. Suffering is what you add: the resistance, the story, the meaning assigned to the hurt. Murakami put the distinction plainly. Singer went further: you are not the voice in your head. !!You are the one who hears it.!! That gap is narrow, and almost everything follows from whether you notice it.
