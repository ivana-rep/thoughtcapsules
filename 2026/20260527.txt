In a toga, one purple thread gives the whole cloth its character. Epictetus used this as an image for how to live in difficult times: not by fixing everything around you, not by retreating from it, but by being that thread. Your particular place, tended with full attention. Each [small act done well](post.html?p=2026/20260519.txt) holds its color regardless of the conditions around it. !!What surrounds you isn't yours to fix. What you do within reach is.!!
