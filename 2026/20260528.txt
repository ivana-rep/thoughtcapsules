What you attend to consistently, you become. Not as metaphor. The myths encode it, the data confirm it. William Clifford argued that beliefs carry moral weight because they motivate action. The same holds for attention. !!You don't choose what you become. You choose what you [attend to](post.html?p=2026/20260523.txt), and the becoming follows.!!
