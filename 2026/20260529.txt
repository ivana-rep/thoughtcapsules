A man inherits seventeen elephants to be divided: half, a third, a ninth. No clean division exists. A neighbor lends one more.
Eighteen splits cleanly: nine, six, two. The borrowed elephant is returned.
!!Some things cannot be owned and cannot be kept. But without them, nothing divides.!!
