Loneliness isn't a failure of connection. It's the structural condition of being finite and conscious. We are brief, sentient anomalies in a universe that has no record of us. [Real connection](post.html?p=2026/20260520.txt) can soften this; the digital substitute often only clarifies the gap by contrast. !!What cures loneliness is presence. What simulates it is noise.!!
