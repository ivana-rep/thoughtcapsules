Most time is wasted in one of two ways: sluggishness when nothing feels urgent enough to start, or [anxiety](post.html?p=2026/20260106.txt) when everything does. The antidote to each is a posture. When you need to act: move as if you're already late — not in panic, but with purpose. When you need to wait: wait as if you're already early — calm, without the cost of impatience.
!!Between the two, there's no room left for either failure.!!
