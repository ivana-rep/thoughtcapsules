A principle is easy to learn and hard to use well. The words are clear — but the judgment of when it applies, how much weight to give it, which version of it fits — [that only comes from having been wrong about it](post.html?p=2026/20260301.txt) in real situations.
!!A principle without experience behind it is an aphorism aimed at the wrong moment.!!
