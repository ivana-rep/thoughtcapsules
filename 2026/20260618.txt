Resilience is the capacity to return to your original shape after a blow. It's valuable — but it's not the same as being structured in a way that requires the blow. Some things don't recover from [pressure](post.html?p=2026/20260614.txt). They develop through it.
!!The fire doesn't survive its fuel. It becomes itself through consuming it.!!
