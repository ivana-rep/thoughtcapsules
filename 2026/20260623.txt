The feeling rarely comes before the action. You don't wait until you feel ready and then begin. You begin, and something shifts. This is also why reliability compounds: once someone knows you'll [show up](post.html?p=2026/20260219.txt), you become their first call without asking for it. Half-interested never catches up with obsessed, not because obsession is dramatic, but because small daily advantages multiply.
