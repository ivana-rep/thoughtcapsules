The paralysis that comes with ambitious goals usually isn't fear of the goal. It's a scaling error: you're measuring the first step against the size of the whole thing. Motivation is fragile and disappears under pressure. Momentum is different. It builds only from something [small enough](post.html?p=2026/20260108.txt) to actually start today. !!The first step's only job is to break the inertia.!!
