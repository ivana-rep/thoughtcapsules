There are three stages to mastery. First: learn the rules. Second: beat others at their own game. Most people stop here, and that's already hard. The third is different: stop optimizing within the existing game and start defining a new one. Picasso didn't paint better portraits; he changed what a portrait could be. !!In stage two you're competing. In stage three you've made competition the wrong question.!!
