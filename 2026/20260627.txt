2026_06_27 | Two kinds of impatience
↳ [back to full archive](2026/2026_full-archive.html)
↳ [back to index](index.html)

There are two kinds of impatience. One is impatient for results: every day is a cost paid toward a future milestone. The other is impatient for the work itself: every day is progress, and the process is already the reward. The first makes you endure the present waiting for something to change. !!The second has no waiting in it.!!
