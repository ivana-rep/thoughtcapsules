The usual sequence is: get the result, then become the person. Succeed, then feel like someone who succeeds. But this reverses the actual direction. The result follows identity, not the reverse. You don't wait to succeed in order to feel like someone who can. You become, as fully as possible, the person who would succeed: in how you think, how you work, what you tolerate. The results tend to follow that. !!Waiting for the outcome to [change who you are](post.html?p=2026/20260422.txt) is waiting for the effect to produce the cause.!!
