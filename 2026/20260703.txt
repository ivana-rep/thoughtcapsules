Yarrow grows almost anywhere — in cracks, along roadsides, in neglected fields. Not because it worked harder than plants that need careful tending. The conditions were right, and it's built to take advantage of them.

Aristotle thought external goods — time, health, resources, circumstances of birth — were part of flourishing, not incidental to it. The story that success belongs entirely to effort alone leaves out most of what made it possible. !!What you did with the conditions matters. That they were present wasn't up to you.!!
