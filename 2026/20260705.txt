There are two ways to watch someone else succeed. One measures the distance between you and them, and the gap feels like a verdict. The other sees proof that the thing is possible — and something loosens.

The difference isn't in what happened. It's in whether you've learned to treat another's success as evidence against scarcity. !!Genuine pleasure at someone else's win [doesn't cost you anything](post.html?p=2026/20260620.txt). The envy does.!!
