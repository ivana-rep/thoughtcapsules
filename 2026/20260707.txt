Extremism isn't measured by where a view falls on the spectrum. Someone can hold radical positions without being an extremist: they update when evidence arrives, tolerate not knowing, can state the strongest case against their own side. The psychology matters more than the content.

The extremist is the person who can't let the belief be questioned — moderate view or radical one. A centrism held with total rigidity is more dangerous than a radicalism held with intellectual humility. !!The tell isn't the position. It's the grip.!! [Certainty](post.html?p=2026/20260412.txt) forecloses; conviction is different.
