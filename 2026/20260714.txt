The quiet test: can you be alone, not lonely but truly alone, without reaching for something to fill the space? It's a different question from whether you like people. You can enjoy other people enormously and still be unable to bear yourself.

A lot of what passes for sociability is escape. A calendar packed with other people is often not generosity but avoidance: company as a way of not sitting with your own thoughts. The ones who don't need it that way tend to give it better.

!!The people who can be alone well tend to offer the best company. They're choosing to be there, not hiding there.!!
