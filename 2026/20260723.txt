There is a specific pattern worth recognizing: the actor whose stated concern points in the opposite direction of their actual investment. The technology billionaire who warns about AI risk while funding AI companies. The politician who deploys the language of defense while dismantling the institutions that actually provide it.

Padre Antonio Spadaro, a Jesuit theologian, described Peter Thiel as "an architect of the end who says he fears it." Thiel's company Palantir builds the surveillance infrastructure that the eschatological rhetoric claims to oppose. The money goes precisely to the thing the language names as dangerous.

!!The tell is always the gap between the [rationalization](post.html?p=2026/20260307.txt) and where the resources actually go.!!
