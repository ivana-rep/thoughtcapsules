# Lessons

Daily log of what I’m meant to learn from each day, written to make experience explicit instead of wasted.

1. Learn also from bad models and examples
2. Become obsessed by who you are and you want to be
3. Beliefs beat willpower 
4. Separate actions from feelings, sometimes things just must be done
5. You will fail for sure, make sure you’re failing for the right reasons 
6. Notice every good little thing
7. Plan, have systems in place 
8. Simple doesn’t mean ineffective 
9. The way out of suffering is through it
10. Everything is a decision that affects your future
11. Don't allow short-term relief to cause long-term damage
12. Start. It usually makes you see things easier than you think
